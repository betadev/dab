Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

When you play a song on any music streaming app, the artist / creator earns some money (e.g. artists like Drake, J Balvin and Post Malone earned $53 million, $40 million and $37 million respectively from Spotify alone in 2020).

Artists at Donate a Beat also create albums with a variety of music for you to enjoy. The money earned from streaming our music is donated to your favorite charitable cause.

More streams = More donations so don’t forget to share so we can donate more.

Financial Tranparency - how and where is the money spent?

80% of the money earned is donated to charitable organizations while 20% is used to support artists as well as our operational & admin costs.

For donating money, we partner with internationally recognized and accredited charities. Our team carefully vets cases so as to achieve the best value for money / impact while donating for each cause.

We share detailed updates on all financial transactions in our newsletter to ensure full transparency. You can subscribe to our newsletter and view past updates here.

What causes can I support through Donate a Beat?

Currently, you can support the following causes:

  • Animal Welfare: This includes support related to food, shelter, medical treatment, adoption and more for homeless / abandoned animals.
  • Climate Protection: This includes support for efforts that aim to reverse or prevent climate change like preservation of forests, oceans, rivers and other natural resources.
  • Disaster Relief: This includes support for people affected by some disaster (like hurricanes, floods, earthquakes) or pandemic (like covid-19).
  • Kids Welfare: This includes support related to education, nutrition, adoption, healthcare and more for children.
  • General Welfare: This includes support for any deserving charitable causes.
What kind of music do you create?

Our first album contains music suitable for a variety of moods such as relaxed, upbeat, focused and workout.

In addition, we are continuously working to expand our selection of music. Please subscribe to our newsletter to hear about new album releases.

Please continue streaming our music to help make this possible.

When should I use Donate a Beat?

Anytime you like. Donate a Beat contains music suitable for a variety of moods like relaxed, focused, upbeat and workout. Just choose the appropriate music that goes along with your current activity.
