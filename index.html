Getting started is so easy, even Grandpa George can do it.

Just follow these 4 steps and you'll be done in less than a minute.

Step 1

Search and subscribe to "Donate a Beat" on your preferred music streaming app.

We're present on all your favorite apps like Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube Music, Prime Music and more.

view complete app list

Step 2

As you explore our album(s), you'll notice that every track mentions a charitable cause that it is linked to.

Simply add all the tracks you like to your playlist.

Step 3

whew..the hard part is over!

Now just enjoy the (amazing) beats made by our artists as you relax, focus, workout or go about your daily routine.

Every time you stream our music, we receive a tiny bit of money which is then donated to the cause mentioned in the track name.

Step 4

Subscribe to our newsletter to join the Donate a Beat community & receive the latest updates
