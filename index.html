Here's how this "magic" works

Artists at Donate a Beat create a variety of music for you to enjoy as you relax, focus, workout or go about your daily routine.

Every time you listen to one of our songs, streaming apps like Spotify pay us a royalty (a fee to use our music). We donate this royalty to the cause mentioned in each song’s title.

So, add our music to your playlist and start listening to support your favorite cause. It’s really that easy to ‘donate a beat’.
